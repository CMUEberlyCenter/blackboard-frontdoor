Blackboard Downtime Alert!

Interim solution to fix system issues affecting Blackboard

Due to recent issues with Computing Services' back-end systems (i.e., Storage Area Network technology failures) that have been causing Blackboard to go down, we are scheduling a necessary downtime window to put into place an interim solution that should improve system performance this semester.

This outage will occur from 12:30am-5:00am on March 2 (just after Midnight on Saturday morning).

We apologize for the recent series of Blackboard outages and for any disruption to your academic work this may have caused. The stability and security of the Blackboard service is a number one priority for us and we are working to remediate the situation.
