Blackboard Help Holiday Hours

While the Blackboard system is monitored 24/7 including over the holidays, please be aware that your Blackboard Help team will be out of the office and not responding to incoming support requests during the official CMU holidays which include: December 24, 25, 31, and January 1.

In addition, the office will be closed for walk-ins on December 22, 23, 26, 29, and 30, but we will be responding to bb-help requests for support as well as accepting and processing incoming course requests.

Thank you and Happy Holidays!
