Make a Course Available

Students won't see a course until the instructor makes it available:

  1. Within the course's Control Panel, select "Customization" > "Properties".
  2. Select "Yes" under "Make Course Available".
  3. Click "Submit".

Known issue: IE 10 and the Grade Center

Several instructors have reported that when using Internet Explorer 10, the Grade Center does not display properly.

IE 10 shows only the header row in the Grade Center and the student rows appear missing.

We have reported this bug to Blackboard. In the meantime, we suggest you try one of two possible workarounds:

  1. Change the "Document Mode" in IE10. See How
  2. Or, use a different browser; we suggest Firefox.

Enroll a TA Manually

  1. From the "Control Panel", select "Users and Groups" > "Users".
  2. Click "Enroll User" > "Find Users to Enroll".
  3. Enter the TA's Andrew ID under Username and change the role dropdown to "Teaching Assistant".
  4. Click "Submit".
